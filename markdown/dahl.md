
# Easy Red Lentil Dahl

## 🧑‍🍳 Ingredients

- [ ] 🧅 1 large onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 🧄 3 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 🍅 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 🌶️ 1 tbsp turmeric
- [ ] 🥥 1 can (400ml) coconut milk or 200ml coconut cream
- [ ] 🌱 2 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 🧂 2 tsp garam masala
- [ ] 🫘 1 cup (200g) red split lentils, rinsed
- [ ] 🧊 1 vegetable bouillon cube
- [ ] 💧 2 cups (500ml) water or vegetable broth
- [ ] 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
- [ ] 🧂 Salt and pepper, to taste

## 🍳 Instructions

- [ ] Add olive oil to a large pan over medium heat.
- [ ] Add onions and stir until translucent (about 5 minutes).
- [ ] Add garlic, turmeric, cumin, and garam masala; cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
- [ ] Add red split lentils, vegetable bouillon cube, and water/broth. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
- [ ] Add coconut milk or cream and stir well.
- [ ] Simmer for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
- [ ] Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 more minutes.
- [ ] Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
- [ ] Serve with rice or naan bread.
