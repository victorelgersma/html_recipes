## 🧑‍🍳 Mise en place
* [ ] 🍚 Rice, rinsed and ready to boil
* [ ] 🍆 Aubergine, cut into bite-sized cubes
* [ ] 🧅 Onion, sliced into thin half-moons
* [ ] 🥩 Ground Beef, room temperature
* [ ] 🫙 Soy Sauce & Oyster Sauce, whisked together (2:1 ratio)
* [ ] 🫒 Olive Oil, for the pan

---

## 🍳 Instructions
1. [ ] Start the rice following package instructions so it's ready for serving.
2. [ ] Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté aubergine cubes until soft and browned; remove and set aside.
3. [ ] In the same pan, brown the ground beef and onions together until meat is cooked through.
4. [ ] Return the aubergine to the pan and pour over the sauce mixture.
5. [ ] Toss on high heat for 1–2 minutes until the sauce is thick and glossy.
6. [ ] Serve hot over the prepared rice.

Pro tip: serve with a [boiled egg](./recipe.php?name=eggs)
